The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the munch larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sence of smell and superior tracking instinct, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports ans foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent ans a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
